THE PANDA SKULL LEGACY

The Panda Skull Coin represents the duality of our brand—fierce yet playful, deadly yet stylish. Originally created for the gaming stream, this emblem has become the signature mark of Heatheringtonsons streetwear. Every piece carries the spirit of the skull: bold, unapologetic, and forever iconic.

THE BRAND

HEATHERINGTONSONS is an independent streetwear label based in Seattle, Washington. We specialize in bold, embroidered apparel that speaks before you do.

Every piece is crafted on premium heavyweight blanks—no cheap materials, no compromise. From the "Riel One" hoodie to our statement snapbacks, we stitch attitude into every thread.

We ship fast from Hialeah, Florida. Unisex fits. Built to last.
